Welcome to PauseLock!

The YouTube Timer that keeps you watching

What is PauseLock?

PauseLock is a Chrome extension that helps you commit to watching YouTube videos by adding a timer during which you cannot pause after starting a video. It's perfect for content creators, podcast enthusiasts, or anyone who wants to reduce the habit of stopping videos midway.

Key Features

  • Customizable Timer - Set how long you must watch before pausing (1-3600 seconds)
  • Friendly Notifications - See how much time remains before you can pause
  • Smart Activation - Option to only enable on videos longer than 5 minutes
  • Ad Awareness - Option to not count ad time toward your timer requirement

How to Use

  1. Click the PauseLock icon in your Chrome toolbar to open the settings
  2. Set your desired timer duration (in seconds)
  3. Customize other settings according to your preferences
  4. Visit YouTube and start watching videos!

When you start a video, the timer activates. If you try to pause before the timer ends, the video will automatically resume playing.
