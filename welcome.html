What is NoFlake?

NoFlake is a Chrome extension that helps you commit to watching YouTube videos by adding a buffer time during which you cannot pause after starting a video. It's perfect for content creators, podcast enthusiasts, or anyone who wants to reduce the habit of stopping videos midway.

How to Use

  1. Click the NoFlake icon in your Chrome toolbar to open the settings
  2. Set your desired buffer time (in seconds)
  3. Customize other settings according to your preferences
  4. Visit YouTube and start watching videos!

When you start a video, the buffer timer activates. If you try to pause before the buffer time ends, the video will automatically resume playing.
